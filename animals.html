Lion

Download Image

Lions are powerful predators known as the "king of the jungle."They live in groups called prides and highly social animal. Lions Play an important role in balacing ecosystems by controlling herbivore population.

  • Habitat:Savannas and Grass lands
  • Diet:Carnivore
  • Lifes span:12-15 years
Speed 80 km/h
Weight 190 kg

Elephant

Download Image

The elephant is the largest land mammal. It has large ears and along trunk. Elephants are herbivoresand live in herds. They have excellent memory.

  • Habitat: Forest and savanna
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Lifespan: 60-70 years
Speed 40 km/h
Weight 6000 kg
