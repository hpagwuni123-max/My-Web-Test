Animals play a vital role in Earths ecosystems - from pollinating plants and maintaining healthy soils, to controlling pest populations and supporting food webs. Understanding animals helps us protect biodiversity and keep ecosystems resilient.

Beyond ecological importance, animals are central to many cultures, economies, and scientific discoveries. Studying them teaches us about evolution, behavior, and how to live sustainably with the natural world.

What visitors can learn

  • How different species adapt to their environments and survive
  • The roles animals play in ecosystems and food chain
  • Conservation challenge and how to protect wildlife.
  • Interesting behaviors and comunication techniques used by animals.
